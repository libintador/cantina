faq

Got a question you're itchin' to ask? Maybe we've already anticipated it. Check below.

How long will I need to wait for my order?

All orders take a day to be ready.

What is a tray's serving size?

A whole tray of food can satisfy 10 - 12 grown ups.

Can I order per piece/slice?

All dishes are to be ordered per tray.

Would I need to send in my own tray for my order?

The tray would already be included in the order price.

Do your tacos travel beyond Bacolod?
